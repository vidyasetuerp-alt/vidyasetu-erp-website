Features

Everything your administration team needs in one ERP.

VidyaSetu ERP brings academic, finance, employee, communication, and reporting workflows into a clean platform designed for speed.

Student Management

Centralize student profiles, documents, guardians, and academic history.

Admission Management

Track enquiries, admissions, forms, and class allocation with ease.

Guardian Information

Maintain parent details, contacts, relationships, and communication records.

Employee Management

Manage staff records, departments, joining details, and documents.

Teacher Management

Organize teacher profiles, subjects, classes, and responsibilities.

Salary Management

Handle payroll, deductions, advances, and salary reports.

Fee Collection

Collect fees, print receipts, apply discounts, and monitor dues.

Attendance Management

Examination Management

Dashboard & Analytics
